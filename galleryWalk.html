Physiology Exhibit

Our Eyes Take Shortcuts

In my UI class, we had a day where each student prepared a presentation on a design related to physiology. My topic was about how our brains make guesses and create an image based on our past experiences. So with design, our brains will automatically fill in shapes if they look like a shape we've seen often, even if the shape isn't closed. You can influence how people visually interpret things because of this.
