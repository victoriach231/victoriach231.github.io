How to prep for a UI/UX interview

1. Research the company, the position, and find out what kind of projects they’ve been working on. Tailor your answer to the company’s needs

2. Prep your portfolio to show off your skills. Make sure your portfolio has projects that meet job description requirements/skills needed

3. Prep for general interview questions: tell me about yourself, how you got into ui/ux design. Use the STAR method to answer: describe the situation, task, action, and the result of the work you did.

4. Prep for technical problems. For example, describe a situation where you disagreed with someone from work. What is your go to design process? (ex: double diamond). Talk about group projects and reflect on them, anything you would’ve done differently? How do you incorporate research or data in your work?

5. They may give you a design challenge to see how you approach problems and solve them. Make sure you completely understand the problem before diving in

6. Ask the interviewer questions! See if the company is a good fit for you. Remember to be yourself!
